About SciDay2026

Our Mission

01

Encourage Knowledge Transfer

Facilitate exchange between academic researchers, clinicians, students, and industry professionals to accelerate discoveries.

02

Support Innovation & Entrepreneurship

Connect scientific discoveries with patient development, startup creation, and technology valorization.

03

Strengthen the Algerian Research Ecosystem

Build sustainable networks and identify funding opportunities for cancer research in Algeria and North Africa.

Core Themes

5 Core Research Themes

Interdisciplinary Approach

SciDay2026 uniquely bridges molecular immunology, clinical oncology, computational biology, and biotech entrepreneurship. By convening experts across disciplines under one roof, we catalyse the kind of cross-pollination that turns fundamental discoveries into life-saving therapies — and therapies into scalable enterprises that benefit patients across Algeria, North Africa, and beyond.
